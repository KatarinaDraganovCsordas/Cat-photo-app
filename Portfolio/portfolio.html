About me:

  • Hello there! As you already know, my name is Katarina, a proud millennial born in '95. I'm originally from Serbia, but currently living in Austria.
  • My journey began as a swimmer. From 2008 to 2015, I proudly represented Serbia as a member of the national swimming team. My most significant accomplishments include setting a national/Balkan record and reaching the finals at the European Championships."
  • Alongside my athletic pursuits, I attended both elementary school and gymnasium
  • In the summers, between swimming seasons and school, I worked as a waitress, babysitter, and swimming teacher.
  • In 2015, I experienced an accident that resulted in paralysis. The same year I moved to Austria.
  • In 2016-2017, I spent time in hospitals and rehabilitations, where I also started learning german.
  • In 2018, I resumed swimming and successfully completed my first German course at the B1 level.
  • In 2019, I achieved the Paralympic qualification time and rejoined the national team (this time for paraswimming). I completed my second German course at the B2 level. And I also began my studies in psychology.
  • 2020 was a challenging year for all of us, so let's skip over it.
  • In 2021, I participated in the Paralympic Games and achieved 9th place.
  • In 2022, I secured the 4th place in the World Championships, and concurrently, I made the decision to discontinue my studies.
  • In 2023, I found myself feeling a bit lost, to be honest. However, towards the end of the year, I discovered the art of coding, web design, and 3D art. Since then, I haven't missed a day, dedicating myself to learning by watching hundreds of hours on YouTube, seeking ways to improve faster. And I also earned two certifications on the FreeCodeCamp website.
