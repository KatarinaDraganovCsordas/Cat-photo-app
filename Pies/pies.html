    Farmstead activities

  • Harvest (fruit picking)

    Come pick fresh fruits in our orchards! Ripe apples, juicy berries, and sweet strawberries are ready for you. Enjoy the simple pleasure of harvesting the best nature has to offer at our farm. It's a fun and delicious experience for everyone!

  • Animal Joy

    At our farm you can feed, pet, and even milk our friendly cows. Don't miss the chance to gather freshly laid eggs from our cheerful chickens, adding an extra layer of excitement to your farm adventure. It's a simple and delightful way to connect with nature, bringing smiles and laughter to visitors of all ages. Join us for a heartwarming day of hands-on farm fun!

  • Baking

    Following each delightful activity, step into our kitchen and transform your harvest into our scrumptious pies. From freshly picked fruits to milk and eggs, the joy of hands-on farming seamlessly transitions into the pleasure of creating and indulging in delectable treats. Join us in turning your farm adventure into a culinary celebration!

    Premium ingredients

  • Flourishing Fields:

    Our farm's fertile fields yield the finest grains and cereals, ensuring the production of exquisite flour for our homemade pies. Nurtured by the sun-drenched soil and cultivated with care, each batch of flour tells a story of quality that begins in our flourishing fields.

  • Happy Hens, Rich Eggs:

    Our commitment to ethical farming practices is evident in the happy and healthy hens that grace our farm. Their well-being translates into eggs of exceptional richness, providing the perfect foundation for our delectable pie crusts.

  • Orchard Treasures:

    From the boughs of our orchards hang the jewels of our pie fillings ‚Äì plump, sun-kissed berries bursting with flavor. Handpicked at the peak of ripeness, these vibrant berries infuse our pies with a burst of seasonal goodness that reflects the essence of our farm.

  • Dairy Delights:

    The heart of our dairy offerings lies in the contented cows that graze our pastures. Their milk, rich and creamy, forms the base for luscious components in our pies. From velvety custards to silky sauces, our dairy delights are a testament to the quality of life our cows enjoy.
